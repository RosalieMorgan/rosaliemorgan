Diet and Nutrition Resources

The Ohio State University Veterinary Medical Center Calorie Calculator
Provides a starting point for estimating a dog's calorie needs, and information regarding body condition scoring.
Association of American Feed Control Officials (AAFCO)
Establishes nutrient standards for dogs. Check food packaging for the AAFCO statement that indicates it meets the standards for the dog's life stage and size.
BalanceIT
Provides an online tool to formulate recipes for home made food appropriate for long-term feeding, and sells supplements to balance the formulas. Poorly balanced diets can cause long term harm before deficiencies will show up in blood work, so it is important to put in the work, and speak with a vet, to make sure any long term diet is properly balanced before feeding it to a dog.

Health Resources

ASPCA Toxic and Non-Toxic Plant List - Dogs
Provides information about symptoms and severity, as well as details about which parts of the plant are toxic.
Merck Veterinary Manual
Includes a Chocolate Toxicity Calculator, and is a wealth of reliable health information.
